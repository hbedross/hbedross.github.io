Imagining the Gorge

"What If"

Graduate Urban Design Studio III

The second phase of the studio focuses on the development of a number of hypotheses for the Gorge Waterfront through narrative drawings. By posing the question “What if?” the goal is set in motion productive ways of thinking about the future of the Gorge Waterfront in order to imagine a variety of site approaches. The project was done in a group of three to develop future typologies to a fairly privatized area of the Gorge Waterway, affected different municipalities in Vancouver Island, British Columbia.
